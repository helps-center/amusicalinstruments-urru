​Privacy Policy

1. What We CollectWe may collect the following types of information:
  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, postal address
  • Donor Information: Donation amounts, payment method (processed securely)
  • Volunteer/Participant Data: Interests, availability, or event signups
  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser, pages visited (via cookies and analytics)
We only collect what’s needed to support our mission and improve your experience.

2. How We Use ItWe use your information to:
  • Keep you informed about our work, updates, or events
  • Process donations and send receipts
  • Communicate with you regarding your involvement
  • Improve our website and outreach efforts
  • Comply with any applicable legal obligations
We always aim to be clear, respectful, and never intrusive.

3. Sharing Your InformationWe do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data with:
  • Payment processors (for secure donation handling)
  • Trusted service providers (like email or event tools)
  • Regulatory bodies, only if required by law
All partners are required to protect your data in line with our values.

4. Data SecurityWe take appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data. This includes secure hosting, encryption of sensitive information, and restricted access.

5. Your Rights & ChoicesYou have the right to:
  • Request access to your personal data
  • Ask for corrections or deletion
  • Withdraw consent from communications
  • Opt out of cookies via browser settings


6. Children’s PrivacyWe do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have, please notify us and we will take appropriate action.

7. CookiesWe use cookies to:
  • Understand how people use our website
  • Improve functionality and user experience
  • Track anonymous visitor behavior
You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

8. Policy UpdatesWe may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the law or our operations. Any updates will be posted here with the new effective date.
